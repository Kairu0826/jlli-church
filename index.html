About Us

Our Story, Mission & Beliefs

We are a Bible-believing church committed to knowing Christ and making Him known. Our heart is to worship God, disciple believers, and serve our community through prayer, compassion, and outreach.

Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus and equip them for ministry.

Vision

A Spirit-empowered church that shines God’s light in families and the nations.

Core Beliefs
  • Authority of Scripture
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
  • Life in the Spirit, prayer, and discipleship
